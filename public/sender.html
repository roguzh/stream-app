Stream to TV

Higher presets need more from both the Mac's encoder and the TV's decoder. If the receiver's dropped-frames stat climbs after switching, its decoder can't keep up — step back down a preset.
Sharing the entire screen or a window doesn't include app audio on Mac — pick a virtual audio device (e.g. BlackHole) here to send system audio too. Sharing a Chrome tab captures that tab's audio natively; leave this on "No extra audio".
